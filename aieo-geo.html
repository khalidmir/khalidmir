AI Engine Optimization
AIEO / GEO

Search is changing.

People are no longer only searching through traditional engines. They are asking questions inside AI systems.

This changes how visibility works. It is no longer only about ranking pages. It is about being understood.

Definition

What is AIEO / GEO

AI Engine Optimization, also referred to as Generative Engine Optimization, focuses on how content, identities, and ideas are interpreted by AI systems.

The focus shifts to:

Clarity of identity
Consistency of ideas
Structured information
Context across platforms

The goal is simple: Make it easy for AI systems to understand who you are, what you do, and how to represent you.

Significance

AI systems do not just index pages. They summarize, interpret, and present information.

If your identity is unclear, your work will be misrepresented or ignored. If your signals are consistent, your presence becomes stronger across AI assistants, search engines, and content summaries.
